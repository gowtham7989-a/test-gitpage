ETL Pipeline Architecture Flow

A visual breakdown of data integration and analytics.

This diagram represents a complete end-to-end Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) pipeline. It starts with data stored on-premises and moves it through a series of stages to make it available for business intelligence and reporting.

Overview of the Pipeline:

Source: On-Premises SQL Server.
Ingestion & Staging: ETL Orchestration and FTP Server.
Transformation: Staged in a Data Lake.
Destination: Parallel Data Warehouse (PDW) and Qlik BI platform.
Consumption: Reporting and Analysis.
